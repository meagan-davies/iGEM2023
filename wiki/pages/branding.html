Overview

CholesterLock's brand identity embodies both modernity and compassion, resonating with our dedication to innovation and the well-being of our patients. With commitment to fostering hope and optimism, we invite patients to join us on a journey to better health and brighter future through innovative biotechnology. Together, we are refining the way high cholesterol is managed, transforming lives with a touch of empathy and promise of vitality.

Project Name

To enhance brand recognition and comprehension, we chose to give our project a descriptive name that captures the nature of the solution. After thorough deliberation, our team reached a unanimous verdict: our innovative fusion protein, and the entire initiative behind it, would be christened "CholesterLock." This name is elegantly straightforward, offering a clear and immediate understanding of our project's purpose. It symbolises the core function of our fusion protein â€“ locking the cholesterol receptors and blocking cholesterol synthesis and absorption.

Colour Palette

Our colour palette, consisting of serene dark blue, calming blue, deep maroon red, warming dark orange, vibrant orange, and yellow, resembling sunrise colours with aims to evoke a sense of healing and renewal.
